Who We Are

About Us

Every student deserves personalized attention and a clear roadmap to success. Our mission is to build strong foundations and make learning intuitive for every aspirant.

VIP Academy is built on a simple belief: with the right guidance from Classes 6–10, every aspirant can crack NEET and JEE. We build strong fundamentals in all subjects and develop the problem-solving skills needed for top-tier exams. Our foundation program focuses on clarity and strategic practice so every student reaches their full potential.

Along with NEET and JEE, we also coach students for a wide range of national entrance exams including: SITEEE, SNUSAT+APT, SRMJEE (UG), AEEE, KIIT-EE, JEE Advanced, BITSAT, VITEEE, CUET, MET, IISER, IISC, AIIMS-EE and JIPMER-EE.
